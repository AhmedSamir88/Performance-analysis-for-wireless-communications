The codes for paper
B. Zhao, C. Zhang, W. Yi and Y. Liu, "Ergodic Rate Analysis of STAR-RIS Aided NOMA Systems", IEEE Communications Letters; 2022.

'analytical.m' is for the analytical results in Section III.
'simulation_STAR_RIS.m' is the simulation for STAR-RIS under uncorrelated and correlated channels in Section IV.
'simulation_RIS.m' is the simulation for traditional RIS in Section IV.
